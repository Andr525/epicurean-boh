Payroll — New York City
Hours, NYC tip-credit wages, and overtime — gross pay calculation for payroll export
2026 NYC rates applied automatically: Tipped food service workers — $11.35/hr cash wage + $5.65/hr tip credit (total $17.00 min). Tipped service employees — $14.15/hr cash + $2.85/hr tip credit. Non-tipped staff, managers, and sales — full $17.00/hr minimum, no tip credit. Overtime (over 40 hrs/week) is calculated at 1.5× the full minimum wage for tipped roles, per NYC law — not the lower cash wage.
